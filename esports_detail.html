My hobbies

Dragons Unfold

In my childhood I was engaged in playing games. During the various LAN parties I visited, I came into contact with graphic design. I have designed logos, corporate identities and t-shirts for a large number of teams.

At the moment I only do branding for major streamers and also for e-sports clubs and organizations that help gamers achieve their dreams. I do this to contribute to the community that has also supported me.

Panetelaa

Livestream overlay and logo

Twitch.tv/panetelaa

The livestream is mainly about the game Fortnite and in addition, the streamer connects gamers with problems to a community that can help them with depression, anxiety and learn to deal with uncomfortable situations.

At the time, I was asked to create a new layout for the livestream in a playful and childlike way. Unfortunately I was not allowed to change the logo and the various texts around it, but I did add a new face to this brand: “the pan man”.

The design is adjusted seasonally with a day and night scene that I work out with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe XD.
